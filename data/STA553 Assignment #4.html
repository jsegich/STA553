1. Introduction

This dataset is base on just over 20,000 chess games played on the site Lichess.com. The variables include the players ratings, the number of turns, the opening played among others. Here I created a Pie chart, Historgram, Boxplot, and Line Chart to vizualoze some key features of the games in this set.
